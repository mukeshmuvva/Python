Purified Water

Reverse Osmosis or Distilled filtration are processes that create purified water removing essential minerals, like Ca, Mg and Na.
Drinking water sources are important dietary sources of minerals, Ca, Mg and Na because they are “highly bioavailable” - McGill University
Demineralized water "has a definite adverse influence on the animal and human organism.” – World Health Organization
